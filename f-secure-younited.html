One cloud. All your stuff. All the time.

Get your clouds and devices connected and get all your stuff in one secure place. At the heart of the younited cloud is simplified sharing, combined with robust privacy and security.

Run your life with Younited

Engaging and fun personal cloud service

Younited enables you to run your life with ease, wherever you go. Your fun personal cloud is available on your computer, smartphone or tablet. It is defined by the robust security and privacy protection of F-Secure in Finland.

With younited, you can see all your stuff in one single place. You can access, organize and share all your music, photos, docs and movies on any device you use. You'll be able to see what you've got in Facebook, Dropbox, and Google Picasa and what you're sharing on them. In essence, younited is true freedom.

Access everything everywhere.

With younited, you'll always have your stuff with you. Your music, photos and videos, younited on your mobile phone, computer or tablet.

Save securely with stubborn Finns.
